Who I am and What I Do

Leticia Coelho is an interior designer with a background in design and a passion for creating stunning commercial and residential spaces.

Originally from Brazil, she has been living in the UK for nearly a decade, bringing her unique style and attention to detail to every project.

Leticia specializes in transforming spaces into beautiful, functional environments that reflect her clients' personalities and needs.

Commercial Bathroom Refurbishment – Leeds

Located in Leeds, this project showcases the complete transformation of a dated commercial bathroom into a space that blends classic elegance with industrial style. We combined marble-effect surfaces, warm wood details, and matte black fixtures to create a refined yet modern atmosphere. The result is a stylish, functional upgrade that elevates the entire space. Food Court Design – Abu Bakr Supermarket, Batley

For this project, I designed a warm and inviting food court space within Abu Bakr Supermarket in Batley. The goal was to create an environment where people feel welcome and comfortable to gather, eat, and relax. Natural plants were thoughtfully integrated to bring life and freshness into the space, while custom timber lampshades hanging from the ceiling add a beautiful touch of craftsmanship and warmth. The result is a balanced, organic atmosphere that enhances the overall customer experience.
